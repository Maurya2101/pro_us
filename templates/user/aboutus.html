About Us

Urban Service provides a platform that allows skilled and experienced professionals to connect with users looking for specific services.
Once on the platform, our match-making algorithm identifies professionals who are closest to the user's requirements and available at the requested time and date.



Our Vision: Empower millions of professionals worldwide to deliver services at home like never experienced before.

Urban Service is a technology platform offering a variety of services at home. Customers use our platform to book services such as beauty treatments, haircuts, massage therapy,
cleaning, plumbing, carpentry, appliance repair, painting, etc. These services are delivered in the comfort of their home and at a time of their choosing. We promise our
customers a high quality, standardized, and reliable service experience. To fulfill this promise, we work closely with our hand-picked service partners, enabling
them with technology, training, products, tools, financing, insurance, and brand, helping them succeed and deliver on this promise.
